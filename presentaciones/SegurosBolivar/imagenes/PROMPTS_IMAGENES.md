Guardar cada imagen **en esta carpeta** (`TedTalk/imagenes/`) con el nombre exacto indicado.
La presentación las carga automáticamente; mientras no existan, muestra un marcador de posición.

**Estilo común para todas** (añadir al final de cada prompt):
> Flat editorial illustration, clean minimal style, white background, generous negative space,
> limited palette: deep blue (#2a78d6), dark navy (#1c5cab), warm gray, small orange accent (#eb6834).
> No text, no letters, no watermarks. 16:10 aspect ratio.

## 1. `loro-estocastico.png` — slide "Qué es un LLM"
**Prompt:** A giant friendly parrot made of thousands of tiny floating words and probability
bars, perched on a laptop, "speaking" a stream of word-fragments that dissolve into
percentages. Conceptual metaphor for a stochastic language model: eloquent but only
repeating statistical patterns.

## 2. `biblioteca-fantasma.png` — slide "Qué es una alucinación"
**Prompt:** A vast law library where some books on the shelves are translucent, ghostly and
slightly glowing — phantom volumes that look identical to the real leather-bound legal tomes
beside them. A hand reaches for a ghost book that is starting to dissolve into particles.
Metaphor for hallucinated court rulings that look real.

## 3. `guardrail-carretera.png` — slide "Guardrails de evaluación"
**Prompt:** A mountain highway at the edge of a cliff protected by a sturdy modern guardrail;
the road surface is made of subtle circuit-board traces and the guardrail posts are small
measuring instruments / gauges. A car drives safely along the curve. Metaphor: measurable
safety barriers for AI systems.

## 4. `datos-fronteras.png` — slide "Transnacionalidad de la información"
**Prompt:** A stylized world map at night where glowing streams of documents and chat bubbles
flow from South America across the ocean toward distant server buildings on other continents;
one stream is stopped by a shield inside an office building (on-premise server), keeping its
glow local. Metaphor for cross-border data flows and local AI models.

## 5. `caja-negra.png` — slide "Derecho a una explicación"
**Prompt:** A person in business attire holding a denied insurance claim document, standing in
front of a huge featureless matte black cube; from the cube's side, a single door is half-open
revealing orderly glowing gears, citations and a magnifying glass inside. Metaphor: opening the
algorithmic black box to obtain an explanation.
